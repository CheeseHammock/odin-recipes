Ants on a Log

Description

Nearly every child born in the 1970s and 1980s is familiar with this horrific fusion. This disgusting snack was likely created by a mother who hated her child. The only thing worse than making your son or daughter this abomination is to pack it in their school lunch so they have to eat it in front of their peers.

Ingredients

  • Celery
  • Peanut Butter
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Wash celery off with water and cut to desired length
  2. Spread peanut butter on top of celery
  3. Put raisins on top of peanut butter
  4. Give them to your child and tell them they taste good so they begin to question your judgement
