Kool-Aid

Description

When your child finally leaves their room after staring at their phone for five hours, why not give them a glass of their favorite artifically colored sugar water? For nearly 100 years, Kool-Aid has been staining carpets and tongues and making sugar consumption fun for the whole family! Kool-Aid is also the official soft drink of the state of Nebraska. Oh yeah!!

Ingredients

  • One Kool-Aid Packet
  • Sugar
  • Water

Steps

  1. Get empty pitcher
  2. Read Kool-Aid packet and dump the appropriate amount of sugar into the pitcher
  3. Empty the Kool-Aid packet contents into the pitcher
  4. Add the required amount of water and stir well with spoon (use the wooden one that mom hits you with)
  5. Pour Kool-Aid into a glass and offer it to your friend (as if you had one)
